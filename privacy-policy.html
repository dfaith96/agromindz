Privacy Policy

How Agromindz handles information used for farmer advisory, crop disease detection, and yield forecasting.

Effective date: June 12, 2026

This sample privacy policy should be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before production use.

1. Information We Collect

Agromindz may collect information provided by farmers, farm managers, agronomists, or website visitors.

  • Contact details such as name, email address, farm or organization name, and location.
  • Farm information such as crop type, field size, soil condition, rainfall condition, planting stage, and yield records.
  • Crop health information such as uploaded leaf images, disease symptoms, pest observations, and treatment notes.
  • Technical information such as browser type, device type, IP address, pages visited, and usage logs.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to provide and improve Agromindz services.

  • Generate farm advisory suggestions, crop disease guidance, and yield estimates.
  • Improve recommendations based on crop conditions, field records, and user feedback.
  • Respond to messages, demo requests, support questions, and service inquiries.
  • Maintain security, prevent misuse, and improve website performance.

3. AI Processing

Agromindz may use AI to analyze crop data, detect disease patterns, summarize farm records, and estimate yields. AI outputs are advisory only and should be confirmed with local agricultural experts when decisions involve chemical use, serious crop disease, food safety, or major financial risk.

4. Image and Field Data

If users upload crop images or field data, Agromindz may process that information to provide disease detection, field advice, or yield forecasting. Users should avoid uploading unnecessary personal information in images or notes.

5. Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers, authorized partners, legal authorities when required, or agricultural support professionals when users request assistance.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. No online system can be guaranteed completely secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and improve platform reliability.

8. Your Choices

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of certain information by contacting Agromindz. Some information may need to be retained for lawful business or security purposes.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact hello@agromindz.space.
