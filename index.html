Product and Service Features

Everything farmers need for smarter field decisions.

Agromindz combines practical advisory tools with AI-powered detection and forecasting services that are easy to use in the field.

A

AI Farm Advisor

The AI Farm Advisor gives farmers practical daily guidance based on crop type, growth stage, soil condition, weather pressure, and visible field problems.

  • Suggests when to irrigate, fertilize, weed, spray, or inspect.
  • Explains recommendations in simple farmer-friendly language.
  • Helps reduce guesswork during planting, growth, and harvest periods.
D

Crop Disease Detection

Crop Disease Detection helps farmers identify plant health problems earlier by reviewing symptoms from leaf photos and field observations.

  • Checks for leaf spots, yellowing, wilting, blight, mold, and pest damage.
  • Separates likely disease signs from nutrient stress or water stress.
  • Provides next-step actions such as isolate, monitor, treat, or call an expert.
Y

Yield Forecasting

Yield Forecasting estimates expected harvest output before the season ends, helping farmers plan sales, storage, transport, and input spending.

  • Uses field size, crop type, rainfall, crop health, and farm practices.
  • Shows likely yield range instead of a single uncertain number.
  • Highlights actions that can still improve final harvest performance.
S

Soil and Nutrient Guidance

Soil and Nutrient Guidance helps farmers understand what their crop may be missing and when nutrients should be applied for better uptake.

  • Recommends compost, manure, lime, or fertilizer timing based on field condition.
  • Connects yellowing, poor growth, and weak roots to possible nutrient issues.
  • Encourages better soil records for future planting seasons.
W

Weather-Aware Planning

Weather-Aware Planning connects rainfall, heat, humidity, and dry spells to the most important farm decisions each week.

  • Guides irrigation planning when moisture is dropping.
  • Warns when humid weather may increase disease pressure.
  • Helps farmers choose safer windows for spraying, planting, and harvesting.
R

Farm Record Insights

Farm Record Insights turns everyday farm notes into useful knowledge for improving the next season and managing costs more carefully.

  • Tracks crop history, planting dates, input use, disease events, and harvest results.
  • Compares what worked across fields and seasons.
  • Helps farmers make better input budgets and production plans.

Crop Disease Detection

Catch crop disease before it spreads.

Agromindz is designed to help farmers spot visible symptoms early, compare likely causes, and take the right next step before yield is affected.

01

Upload or capture a leaf image

Farmers can photograph leaves, stems, or fruit damage for AI-assisted symptom review.

02

Identify disease patterns

The system checks for spots, yellowing, wilting, mold, blight-like marks, pest injury, and nutrient stress.

03

Recommend response actions

Agromindz suggests inspection steps, isolation, watering changes, treatment options, or expert escalation.

04

Track recovery

Farmers can record treatment dates, follow-up photos, and crop response to improve future decisions.
